About Our Institution

Smt. Shakuntala Memorial Charitable Trust ®

Established by founder and president Sri. Dr. Veerabhadrappa E. Gadigi, former Medical Officer, Chittagubbi Hospital, Hubli.

The Institution was started with the aim of "Service to Humanity" along with achievement orientation to the noble profession. Smt. Shakuntala Institute of Nursing Education and Paramedical courses are situated at Garden City - Hubli, about 2 km away from the city Railway Station & about 1 km from the Bus Stand.

Located in Vidyanagar, Hubli, the campus provides a calm, quiet, and natural environment conducive for academic learning.

Admission Guidelines 2026-27

Step-by-Step Admission Process

Follow our simple, transparent 6-step online admission workflow.

01

Online Registration

Create your account and register using your personal details on our official portal or at our Hubli & Kittur admissions office.

02

Application Form

Complete the online admission form carefully with your academic background, course preference, and personal details.

03

Upload Documents

Upload all required academic marksheets (10th/12th/PUC Science), transfer certificates, conduct certificates, and photo ID.

04

Application Review

Our dedicated admission team verifies your documents, academic eligibility, and seat availability as per INC & KNC norms.

05

Fee Payment

Complete the admission fee payment through online banking, UPI, or demand draft to officially reserve your seat.

06

Admission Confirmation

Receive your official admission confirmation letter, hostel allotment details, and campus reporting orientation schedule.

Academic Programs

Programs Offered

Recognized by INC & KNC • Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS).

Degree Program

B.Sc. Nursing

Four-year comprehensive degree program equipping students with in-depth nursing knowledge, clinical skill lab training, and super-specialty hospital rotations.

Duration 4 Years
Eligibility 10+2 Science (PCB) 45%
Intake Capacity 60 Seats
Affiliation INC, KNC & RGUHS
Diploma Program

Diploma in GNM

General Nursing & Midwifery 3-year diploma focusing on bedside care, maternity nursing, emergency care, and community healthcare services.

Duration 3 Years
Eligibility 10+2 / PUC (Any Stream)
Intake Capacity 60 Seats
Affiliation INC & KNC Approved
Diploma Courses

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)

Three-year paramedical diploma training specialists in clinical pathology, medical lab diagnostics, biochemistry, hematology, and microbiological testing.

Duration 3 Years
Eligibility Pass in SSLC and PUC Science
Intake Capacity 20 Seats
Affiliation GOK & KSPMB, Bangalore
Diploma Courses

Diploma in Operation Theatre & Anaesthesia Technology (DOT & AT)

Three-year practical diploma equipping technicians for surgical operation theatre management, anaesthesia assistance, sterilizer operations, and ICU care.

Duration 3 Years
Eligibility Pass in SSLC and PUC Science
Intake Capacity 20 Seats
Affiliation GOK & KSPMB, Bangalore
Diploma Courses

Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology (DMIT)

Three-year paramedical diploma specializing in X-ray imaging, diagnostic radiograph operation, CT scan assistance, and radiation safety protocols.

Duration 3 Years
Eligibility Pass in SSLC and PUC Science
Intake Capacity 20 Seats
Affiliation GOK & KSPMB, Bangalore

Student Residence

A Home That Teaches Too

All admitted candidates stay in the hostel provided by the institution. Separate hostels for male and female students are arranged with adequate facilities. Delicious, wholesome Karnataka meals are available throughout the year.

Residence Guidelines

Hostel Rules & Regulations

01

Staying in the college hostel is compulsory for outstation students.

02

Steel cot, chair with writing pad, tube light, fans, and rack are provided in every room.

03

Four students are accommodated in each room with spacious ventilation.

04

Students should bring their own bedding and personal clothes.

05

Breakfast menu includes Chappathi, Roti, Dosa with vegetable curry as per the hostel mess timetable, prepared in Karnataka style.

06

Sick leave is granted only on the submission of a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner authorized by the institution.

Institutional Leadership

Founder & President

Dr. Veerabhadrappa E. Gadigi

“Our journey began with B.Sc. Nursing and GNM programmes in 2003, which have produced highly skilled nursing professionals serving in hospitals and healthcare institutions across India and abroad.

In 2019, we expanded our academic excellence by introducing Paramedical courses, including Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT), Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology (DOT), Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology (AT), and Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology (DMIT) to meet the growing demand for allied healthcare professionals.”

Secretary

Dr. Vijay V. Gadigi

“Welcome to Smt. Shakuntala Institute of Nursing Education and Smt. Shakuntala Institute of Paramedical Sciences.

Since its establishment in 2003, Smt. Shakuntala Memorial Charitable Trust has been dedicated to providing quality education and creating skilled healthcare professionals with strong ethical values. The Trust started B.Sc. Nursing and GNM programmes in 2003, laying a strong foundation for nursing education in North Karnataka.”
